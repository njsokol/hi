"After many months of planning, the 11 of us trekked our way to Garner State Park for our very first camping trip together. The boys got an early start at the river, while the girls stock piled the necessary food and drinks. We enjoyed some tasty hotdogs the first night and an intense round of Cards Against Humanity with S'mores to boot. Hiking up Mt. Baldy, floating in the river, a great game of Jenga and sitting around the fire on our last night rounded off the trip. Overall, the trip was a great experience. Truly a great time and looking forward to tackling our next trip Big Bend 2015 together!" - Maggie



"Garner was a great getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city. We learned so much about each other in our adventurous escapades! This was my first "real" camping experience. Most of our planned meals didn't work out, so we had to improvise but overall a wild and exciting trip. LOOKING FORWARD TO BIG BEND 2015!!" - Jarvis
